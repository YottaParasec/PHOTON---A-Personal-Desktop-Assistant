Simple Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
3. In a separate bowl, mix together water, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.
4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix.
5. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
7. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes before inverting it onto a wire rack to cool completely.
8. Frost as desired. Enjoy!
